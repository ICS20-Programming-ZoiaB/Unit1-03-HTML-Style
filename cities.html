Capital Cities

Buenos Aires

Buenos Aires is the capital city of Argentina. The city interestingly contains the widest street in the world: Avenida 9 de Julio. Buenos Aires is alwo known for its many bookstores.

Lima

Lima is the capital city of Peru It is the fifth-largest city in South America and is nicknamed "City of the Kings" Lima is famous for its excellent cuisine, being a culinary capital of the Americas.

Reykjavik

Reykjavik is the capital city of Iceland. It's name means "Smoky Bay". This city is full of penguins and is heated using geothermal energy.

Kyiv

Kyiv is the capital city of Ukraine. It is the eighth-largest city in Europe and is known as the "most green city" due to its many parks, woods, and botanical gardens.
